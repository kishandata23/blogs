What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. These machines can perform tasks such as recognizing speech, making decisions, and solving problems.

Why AI Matters

AI is transforming industries from healthcare to finance by automating complex processes, improving efficiency, and creating new opportunities. It helps us analyze vast amounts of data quickly and make smarter decisions.
